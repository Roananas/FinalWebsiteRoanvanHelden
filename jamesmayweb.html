Production

The video production process consists of 3 main steps: pre-production, which is the planning stage for mapping out your strategy and script for the video, production is the phase in which the video is shot, and finally post-production, which involves editing the video, adding music and other effects. We as a company will take care of all the steps. Not only that but we will assist whenever nessesary in script development and casting.

We will work together with the client to answer questions like; What are your objectives?, Who is this video for? And what is your core message? We will then Build a Video Strategy specifically for that content.
